Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 1 tomato, sliced

Instructions:

  1. Place bacon in a large, deep skillet.
  2. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain, and set aside.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Spread butter onto one side of each slice of bread.
  5. Lay 4 slices of bread, butter side down, in the skillet.
  6. Top with a slice of cheese, 2 slices tomato, bacon, and another slice of cheese.
  7. Cover with a slice of bread, butter side out.
  8. Fry sandwiches until golden on both sides.
